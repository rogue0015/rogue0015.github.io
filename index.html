ABOUT BRANDON

FIELD ID // 0001 — GWERU, ZW

Brandon leads and builds tech communities across Zimbabwe and the wider region — helping revitalise Google Developer Groups (GDG) Harare, mentoring student communities through Applied Machine Learning Days Africa and Zindi, and previously serving as a Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador.

His work centres on one problem: AI is advancing fast, but access to it isn't shared evenly. In 2024 he began running language models offline using Ollama and Gemma, and now teaches others to build with AI on the tools and networks they actually have. He's currently studying toward a Master of Commerce in Strategic Management while working as an ICT Graduate at Midlands State University.
